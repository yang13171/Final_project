Frequently Asked Questions


Who can donate?

Anyone can donate!

How can I donate?
You can visit our Donate Now page! Join our community by signing up here!

What should I donate?
In our Shelters page, there is a list of specified food that each shelter takes.

How can I volunteer?
You can visit our Volunteer Today page! Sign up if you are a new volunteer or sign in if you are a returning volunteer.

Why is it important?

We are dedicated to providing accessible nutrients to those in need. Instead of letting tons of food go to waste every day, it will go to those with limited or no access to a healthy meal.

Who benefits from this?
The Philadelphia community benefits from the contributions! Every empty stomach we help feed is one step closer to closing the hunger gap.

About Phil The Shelters


Phil The Shelters is a small organization based in the Philadelphia area that wishes to connect accessible quality food to those in need.
Our organization is conscious of the amount of food waste that occurs in Philadelphia on a daily basis.
We decided to create a safe and resourceful outlet that gives people the ability to help feed their community.
Phil the Shelters aims to fill the shelters of Philadelphia with food.
