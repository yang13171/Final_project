About Phil the Hunger

We are a small organization that wishes to help the community.
Our organization are conscious of the waste of food that happens in Philadelphia.
Therefore, we formed our group project for our program, Phil the Hunger.
We decided to make a website that gives people the ability to help their community.
The reason we picked Phil the Hunger as our name was to fill the shelters of Philly with food.
We wanted to decrease the waste of food around Philly as well as help those in need.
